    neurodsp.aperiodic.dfa.compute_rescaled_range¶

    neurodsp.aperiodic.dfa.compute_rescaled_range(sig, win_len)[source]¶

    Compute rescaled range of a given time series at a given scale.

    Parameters:
    sigarray

    Time series.

    win_lenint

    Window length for each rescaled range computation, in samples.

    Returns:
    rsfloat or 1d array

    Average rescaled range over windows.

    Notes

    • Rescaled range was introduced as a measure of time series variability, by Harold Hurst [1].
