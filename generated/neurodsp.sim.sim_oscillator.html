    neurodsp.sim.sim_oscillator¶

    neurodsp.sim.sim_oscillator(n_seconds, fs, freq, rdsym=0.5)¶

    Simulate an oscillation.

    Parameters:
    • n_seconds (float) – Signal duration, in seconds
    • fs (float) – Signal sampling rate, in Hz
    • freq (float) – Oscillator frequency
    • rdsym (float) –
      Rise-decay symmetry of the oscillator, as fraction of the period in the rise time
      =0.5 - symmetric (sine wave) <0.5 - shorter rise, longer decay >0.5 - longer rise, shorter decay
    Returns:

    osc – Oscillating time series
    Return type:

    1d array
