    API Documentation¶

    Time-Frequency analysis¶

    filt.filter_signal(sig, fs, pass_type, fc[, ...]) Apply a bandpass, bandstop, highpass, or lowpass filter to a neural signal.
    timefrequency.phase_by_time(sig, fs, f_range) Calculate the phase time series of a neural oscillation.
    timefrequency.amp_by_time(sig, fs, f_range) Calculate the amplitude time series.
    timefrequency.freq_by_time(sig, fs, f_range) Estimate the instantaneous frequency at each sample.

    Spectral analysis¶

    spectral.compute_spectrum(sig, fs[, method, ...]) Estimating the power spectral density (PSD) of a time series from short-time Fourier Transform (mean, median), or the entire signal’s FFT smoothed (medfilt).
    spectral.compute_scv(sig, fs[, window, ...]) Compute the spectral coefficient of variation (SCV) at each frequency.
    spectral.spectral_hist(sig, fs[, window, ...]) Compute the distribution of log10 power at each frequency from the signal spectrogram.
    spectral.morlet_transform(sig, freqs, fs[, ...]) Calculate the time-frequency representation using morlet wavelets.
    spectral.rotate_powerlaw(f_axis, spectrum, ...) Change the power law exponent of a power spectrum about an axis frequency.

    Rhythmic analysis¶

    burst.detect_bursts_dual_threshold(sig, fs, ...) Detect periods of oscillatory bursting in a neural signal.
    burst.compute_burst_stats(bursting, fs) Get statistics of bursts.
    swm.sliding_window_matching(sig, fs, ...[, ...]) Find recurring patterns in a time series using the sliding window matching algorithm.
    laggedcoherence.lagged_coherence(sig, ...[, ...]) Quantify the rhythmicity of an oscillator using the lagged coherence measure.

    Signal simulation¶

    sim.sim_filtered_noise(n_seconds, fs, exponent) Simulate colored noise that is highpass or bandpass filtered
    sim.sim_synaptic_noise(n_seconds, fs[, ...]) Simulate a neural signal with 1/f characteristics beyond a knee frequency.
    sim.sim_ou_process(n_seconds, fs[, theta, ...]) Simulate mean-reverting random walk (Ornstein-Uhlenbeck process)
    sim.sim_variable_powerlaw(n_seconds, fs, ...) Generate a power law time series with specified exponent by spectrally rotating white noise.
    sim.sim_oscillator(n_seconds, fs, freq[, rdsym]) Simulate an oscillation.
    sim.sim_noisy_oscillator(n_seconds, fs, ...) Simulate an oscillation embedded in background 1/f noise.
    sim.sim_bursty_oscillator(n_seconds, fs, freq) Simulate a bursty oscillation.
    sim.sim_noisy_bursty_oscillator(n_seconds, ...) Simulate a bursty oscillation embedded in background 1/f noise.
    sim.sim_jittered_oscillator(n_seconds, fs, freq) Simulate a jittered oscillator, as defined by the oscillator frequency, the oscillator cycle, and how much (in time) to jitter each period.
